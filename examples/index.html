The example files listed below are provided to help you understand how to use Shadowbox to accomplish a specific task.

Name Description
Simple Photo Open a photo by clicking on a link
Thumbnail Photo Open a photo by clicking on a thumbnail
Thumbnail Gallery Open a gallery of photos by clicking on any one of several thumbnails
Simple Video Open a video by clicking on a link
Simple Flash Open a Flash SWF by clicking on a link

This file is part of Shadowbox.js.
